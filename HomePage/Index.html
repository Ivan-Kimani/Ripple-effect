ABOUT ME

Empowering Single Parents
One Ripple at a Time

I am not just a coach. I am your ally, your confidante, and your source of unwavering support. Through my own experiences, I have navigated the tumultuous waters of single parenthood, surmounted heartache, and triumphed over seemingly insurmountable odds.

I have raised three incredible children while juggling work, personal growth, and the myriad of roles life has thrown my way.

Services

At Ripple Effect, we provide a comprehensive range of services designed to support single parents in navigating life’s challenges with confidence and resilience

One-on-One Coaching

Personalized guidance to help you tackle parenting challenges, set goals, and thrive in your personal and professional life.

Group Coaching

Connect with a community of like-minded parents, share experiences, and gain valuable insights through collaborative sessions.

Workshops

Interactive workshops focused on skill-building, personal growth, and effective parenting strategies.

Kids Programs

Engage your children in activities that promote emotional well-being, creativity, and positive development.

Group Programs

Join safe, moderated spaces where single parents come together to share stories, offer support, and inspire each other.

Parents & Kids Events

Participate in fun, enriching activities designed to strengthen parent-child relationships and create lasting memories.

Dial-to-Vent

Need someone to talk to? Our confidential, on-demand service offers a compassionate ear and real-time advice when you need it most.
